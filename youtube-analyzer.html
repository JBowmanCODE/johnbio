How It Works

This tool analyzes a YouTube channel's videos by communicating with a secure back-end service, which uses the official YouTube Data API.

  1. You enter a channel URL and keyword and click "Analyze".
  2. The request is sent to a server-side worker, not your browser.
  3. The worker fetches all public videos and Shorts from the channel's main "Uploads" playlist.
  4. It filters this list by searching for your keyword within each video's title.
  5. Finally, it calculates the total stats for all matching videos and sends the result back to this page.
