YouTube Blog Generator

Paste a YouTube URL or transcript — get an SEO-optimized blog post in seconds

help How to get a YouTube transcript

  1. Open the YouTube video you want to convert
  2. Click Show more in the video description
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the description and click Show transcript
  4. The transcript panel opens on the right — click the three dots menu and select Toggle timestamps to turn them off
  5. Select all the transcript text, copy it, and paste it below
