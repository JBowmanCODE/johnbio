What is the EU AI Act?

The EU AI Act (2024/1689) is the world's first comprehensive AI regulation. It classifies AI systems into risk tiers and requires different compliance measures based on the risk level. High-risk systems (like HR tools or credit scoring) must have risk management systems, data governance, transparency measures, and human oversight.
